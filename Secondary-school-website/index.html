subjects we offer

We provide a diverse range of subjects designed to meet the interest and goals of all pupils. whether you're passionate about siences, the arts, or business, our curriculum supports both academic excellence and personal growth. Explore our subjects areas to find out what inspires you.

pure sciences

Our Pure Sciences program includes Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Pupils develop a strong foundation in scientific principles, critical thinking, and practical laboratory skills.

Art & Music

This program nurtures creativity and self-expression through visual arts, music theory, and perfomance. Pupils Explore various techniques, instruments, and artistic styles, prepsring them for creative industries or personal enrichment.

Accounts

Our Accounts program introduces pupils to the basics/fundamentals of financial literacy, bookkeeping, and business principles. It lays the groundwork for careers in accounting, business managent, and enterpreneuship.
